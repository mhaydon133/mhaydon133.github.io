  1. Romance:
    1. Junjo Romantica: This one is a bit older in terms of animation but the story line is good. There are three seasons that follow our main characters through thier lives. There are three other couples that the story follows as well as they interact with the main couple and each other. This anime is not for those who are underage.
    2. Love Stage: Is a one season show of 10 episodes that follow the exploits of two boys as they go through the entertainment industry and discover love. Niether one thought that they wanted anything to do with another guy but it turns out to be the best thing they could both have.
    3. Maid-Sama: Misaki is class president of a formerly all-male school. She rules over the boys with an iron fist and a heart of ice. A popular male student stumbles on to her secret double life- working part-time as an upbeat and cheery waitress at a maid cafe! Misaki is forced to make a deal: spend some time with him, or have her hard earned reputation destroyed by revealing her embarrassing secret.
